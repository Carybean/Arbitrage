What is Arbitrage Betting?

Arbitrage betting, also known as "arbing" or "sure betting," is a technique where you place bets on all possible outcomes of a sporting event at odds that guarantee a profit regardless of the result.

Key Concepts:

  • Risk-Free Profit: Unlike traditional betting, arbitrage eliminates the risk of loss.
  • Market Inefficiencies: Profits come from differences in odds between bookmakers.

Example Scenario:

For a tennis match between Player A and Player B:

  • Bookmaker X offers 2.10 for Player A to win
  • Bookmaker Y offers 2.10 for Player B to win
  • This creates a guaranteed profit margin of 4.76%
